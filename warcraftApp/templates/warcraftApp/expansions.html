Click on the cream buttons to learn more about each expansion:

2022

Dragonflight is released

The age of Dragons! WoW's most recent expansion brings players to the home of dragons and a new zone, the Dragon Isles. Maybe some of Deathwing's corruption remains? Players will also get a new playable race and class combination: the Dracthyr Evoker.

2020

Shadowlands is released

The newest villain emerges, meet Zovaal, aka "The Jailer". Zovaal has been working with another returning villain from Azeroth, Sylvanas Windrunner. Players travel to the realm of death, a new zone, the Shadowlands.

2018

Battle for Azeroth is released

The Alliance and Horde, no longer bound by a common enemy, have begun fighting again. BFA introduces the new zones Zandalar and Kul Tiras, as well as the Allied Races, which consist of 5 playable races for both Alliance and Horde factions.

2016

Legion is released

Legion stars the infamous anti-hero Illidan Stormrage, and the returning villain, Gul'dan. Players explore the new zones, Broken Isles and Argus, and we see a new playable class, demon hunters.

2014

Warlords of Draenor is released

Warlords of Draenor takes players to a new Zone, Draenor. We also see a new villain, Gul'dan, and this won't be the last time players encounter the first orcish warlock.

2012

Mists of Pandaria is released

The Horde and Alliance have landed in a new zone: Pandaria. The fighting between these two factions awakens ancient evils in Pandaria. A new race is introduced, playable for both factions, the Pandaren. In addition, we see a new class, monks.

2010

Cataclysm is released

A new villain takes center stage: Deathwing. Deathwing is a Dragon Aspect, the leader of the black dragonflight, and corrupted by the Old Gods. Two new playable races were introduces: Goblins and Worgen, and several new zones discovered in Azeroth.

2008

Wrath of the Lich King is released

Enter the infamous Lich King, Arthas Menethil. Players return to Azeroth and explore a new zone, Northrend, while attempting to stop the Lich King from conquering Azeroth. Death Knights are the newest class introduced in WOTLK.

2007

The Burning Crusade is released

WoW's first expansion pack. The Burning Crusade took players away from Azeroth to a new world - the Outlands. The Burning Crusade also introduced 2 new races, Blood Elves and Draenei.

2004

WoW (Vanilla) is released

The base game for World of Warcraft. Classic or Vanilla WoW introduces 8 races, 9 classes, and 2 major zones: Eastern Kingdoms and Kalimdor. This is when players see the world of Azeroth for the first time.
